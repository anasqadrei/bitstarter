Hello. Consider this perfect scenario:

  • Jim is going on a trip on March and wants a loan of $600 to spend when he travels.

  • Amanda & Joe are expecting a baby on April and would love to get $600 to pay for the delivery expenses.

  • Peter is taking a course in May that costs $600.

  • Alijandra loves shopping and usually she spends $600 on summer shopping every June.

  • Abdul planning on buying a car in July and wants $600 to pay as down payment.

  • Keasha is moving into a new apartment on August and wants $600 to buy furniture.

  • Lee wants $600 to pay off his credit card in September.

So in this case we have 7 people each need $600 over the period of 7 months. If everyone pays $100 every month to the person in need of money that month, we will end up with everyone getting their $600 loan with 0.0% interest rate.

How it works is that they meet on Social Loans website and they agree that each month they will pay $100 over the 7 month period.

  • On March each of Amanda, Peter, Alijandra, Abdul, Keasha, and Lee would pay $100 to Jim who will get $600 to spend on his trip.

  • On April each of Jim, Peter, Alijandra, Abdul, Keasha, and Lee would pay $100 to Amanda who will get $600 to pay for her new baby costs.

  • On May each of Jim, Amanda, Alijandra, Abdul, Keasha, and Lee would pay $100 to Peter who will get $600 to pay for his course.

  • On June each of Jim, Amanda, Peter, Abdul, Keasha, and Lee would pay $100 to Alijandra who will get $600 to buy summer clothes.

  • On July each of Jim, Amanda, Peter, Alijandra, Keasha, and Lee would pay $100 to Abdul who will get $600 to pay for his new car down payment.

  • On August each of Jim, Amanda, Peter, Alijandra, Abdul, and Lee would pay $100 to Keasha who will get $600 to buy furniture when she move to her new apartment.

  • On September each of Jim, Amanda, Peter, Alijandra, Abdul, and Keasha would pay $100 to Lee who will get $600 to pay off his credit card.

OK, that was the perfect scenario, but how do we reach that? First, you visit our Social Loans website and tell us how much money approximately you need, when do you need it, and how much you are willing to pay each month. This will teach you planning ahead. The website tries to match you with others in order to form a loan circle. It might not be perfect, but if most people in the circle agrees to discuss it then we will open a room or a thread on the website in order for you to discuss and try to set the loan amount, months, and the installments you pay each month. Then if everyone agrees then the website will handle getting the money from everyone and send it to the right person at the right time that everyone set in the loan circle. If it didn’t work, we will set another loan circle for you to discuss with others.

Of course you may not get the perfect loan amount on the perfect time, but that is the downside of a 0.0% interest loan. It would be perfect if more people sign up for the service and would tell us about their future plans that would need money such as traveling, seasonal shopping, getting married, having babies... etc. So tell your friends and family and let’s build a big community to bond and help each other to lower the cost of loans.

FAQ

What if someone in the circle doesn't pay?
Don't worry. We will pay you instead of that person and we will get our money from them by law.
Is it available to the whole world?
No, because each country has different currency. Circles are country or currency based.
Is it a free service?
No, you pay small one time fee when the loan is setup and ready. This will cover our operational costs. We are non-profit organization anyway.
What's the point if I am the last one in the circle? I would pay every month and get my loan after I paid it.
It would be called saving instead of loan. Actually this product is a mix between loan and saving where the first person in the circle gets a loan, the last person saves, and the people in the middle save some then get the loan.
How to set it up?
The same way you setup a Paypal account. i.e. by linking your account to a credit card to bank account or Paypayl.
